Fox Render Farm

Fox render farm is a cloud based rendering service that is based out of Hong Kong. Fox Render Farm has been the industry standard solution for many large companies such as Disney, LucasFilm, and Sony since it's release in 2011. Fox Render Farms green data center runs solely on natural energy. They are dedicated to keeping their carbon emissions low. Fox Render Farm uses their own services to render.

Render Instructions

  • In blender click file>make all paths relative
    or
    file>pack into .blend
  • Ensure all of your assets such as textures are in the one folder you will be uploading
  • In the Fox Render Farm panel click keep local paths at the top
  • Drag your project into the Fox Renderfarm panel
  • Using the select render files button at the left navigate to your project and choose your desired software to render with
  • Set up rendering configuration and add any plug-ins if needed
  • After you submit check the analysis results of your upload

    if it comes back successful you are ready to render

    if it comes back unsuccessful you need to check that you followed all of the steps above
