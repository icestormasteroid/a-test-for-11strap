iRender

iRender is a cloud based rendering service that is based out of Vietnam that focuses on giving the user full control of their projects rendering settings. iRender uses new technology to allow you to remotely connect to a computer and set up your project in that environment. iRender is one of the newest cloud rendering solutions, and it is quickly gaining recognition and popularity. Fox Render Farms green data center runs solely on solar power energy. They are dedicated to keeping their carbon emissions low. iRender uses their own services to render.
